OpenTS

Democratizing AI for Time series and Spatio-temporal data (AI4TS)

OpenTS is an open, comprehensive AI suite designed to make cutting-edge AI4TS research accessible to all. Built on the AGREE principles—Automation, Generalization. Robustness, Explainability, and Efficiency—it empowers researchers and practitioners with:

  • Benchmarks: Standardized evaluations for fair comparisons and reproducible AI4TS research.
  • Models: State-of-the-art AI4TS models, ready for both academic research and real-world deployment.
  • Applications: End-to-end solutions across domains such as AIOps, Intelligent Transportation, Virtual Power Plants, AI4DB, FinTech, and Smart Emergency Response.

Developed by the Decision Intelligence Lab at East China Normal University, in collaboration with the Data Engineering, Science and Systems Group at Aalborg University, OpenTS advances data-driven decision intelligence by transforming complex data into AI-powered decisions, bridging cutting-edge research with real-world impact.

Benchmark

OpenTS offers comprehensive time series benchmark datasets with diverse characteristics from multiple domains and complex settings.

OpenTS covers a diverse range of methods, including statistical learning, machine learning, deep learning methods, and time series foundation models.

TFB Overview

Time series Forecasting Benchmark (TFB) is a comprehensive and fair benchmarking of time series forecasting methods. TFB provides comprehensive time series datasets, rich time series forecasting algorithms, and a unified evaluation pipeline with various strategies and metrics.

Comprehensive dataset

TFB offers comprehensive time series benchmark datasets with diverse characteristics from multiple domains and complex settings.

Rich algorithms

TFB covers a diverse range of methods, including statistical learning, machine learning, and deep learning methods.

Unified Pipeline

TFB offers a unified & flexible pipeline for evaluating forecasting methods, streamlining and standardizing the steps of loading time series datasets, configuring experiments, and evaluating methods.
